Technological Drift

2024

This project explores the disconnection stemming from our limited understanding of the technology we use daily. By examining how computers operate at a fundamental level— through binary logic and operations—I aimed to create a piece influenced by binary numbers in a way that remains hidden from the viewer.

The Chains That Bound Us

2024

This project represents an exploration into the convergent possibilities of projection art and interactive media, investigating how traditional display surfaces can be transformed into responsive, participatory experiences.

Using TouchDesigner as my primary tool, I sought to create a system where viewers transition from observers to active participants in the creative process. My approach draws upon a rich tradition of mixed media installation artists who work at the intersection of light, form, and bodily presence within spatial environments. Daniel Rozin comments “In many cases the viewer becomes the contents of the piece and in others the viewer is invited to take an active role in the creation of the piece.” This is the sentiment I sought to explore.
